My Brother and I

This is a picture of me and my brother, taken a year ago by my mom. He has CFC, a syndrome vaguely similar to Down's and Noonan's. He's a really cool guy, and he's just as into music as I am, so we jam fairly often on guitar and Piano.

Having a brother with an intellectual/disability got me very invested in the ability awareness community, so I like to make a plug for this awesome camp I work for at any and every opportunity I get.
